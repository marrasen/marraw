Develop panel

The right sidebar, in the Studio language. Collapsible sections keep a long adjustment list scannable; each changed slider lights its value teal so you can read your edit at a glance; the neutral panel never competes with the image beside it.

Read your edit

A section header shows a teal summary chip when anything inside is non-zero. Untouched groups stay silent — the panel tells you what you changed without opening every fold.

Center-origin tone

Every tonal slider fills from the middle. Positive pushes right in teal, negative pushes left — direction and magnitude in a single glance across the whole stack.

Pipette white balance

The active pipette glows teal; click a neutral in the photo to set Temp & Tint, or drag the sliders directly. "As Shot" is always one tap away.

Dense, quiet type

Labels in 12px Geist, values in mono tabular so digits never jump. 15px gutters, 4px slider tracks — instrument-tight, never cramped.
